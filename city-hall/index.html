What this page covers

City Hall decisions shape more of everyday life in West Covina than most people realize. Parking rules, development approvals, spending decisions, zoning changes, public safety discussions, and neighborhood planning often start in city council or commission meetings. The problem is that most residents do not have time to sit through long meetings or decode government language.

This page is built to make West Covina City Hall easier to follow. StayJustin breaks down city council meetings, planning commission agendas, public hearings, and important votes in plain language. You will find summaries of what happened, what it means, who it affects, and what to watch next.

Whether you are trying to keep up with a major vote, understand a new proposal, or simply stay informed about how West Covina is being run, this page is your shortcut to the most important City Hall updates.
